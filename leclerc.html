CHARLES LECLERC

The Driver

Charles Leclerc was born in Monte Carlo on October 16, 1997. He quickly became one of the most talented drivers of the modern Formula 1 era. He debuted in Formula 1 in 2018 with Alfa Romeo Sauber and in 2019 joined Ferrari, realizing his childhood dream of racing for the most iconic team in motorsport.
